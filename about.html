About MCC

Mankato Cricket Club was established to spread cricket around the Mankato community and to inspire domestic and international students to take part in the game. Mankato Cricket Club arranges cricket tournament every semester since 2008 and successfully accomplished 24 tournaments with the participation of 19 universities from Midwest. We host the biggest tournament of Midwest in Mankato. We are also a member of ACC (American Collage Cricket). Last few years we are also taking part in other tournaments that were held in different states.

Mankato Cricket Club is a well known cricket club in Minnesota. MCC is a healthy and rapidly growing organization with members from all different countries such as; Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka and Nepal. They have earned the respect of the local community by demonstrating the discipline and conduct required for the game of cricket. To organize cricket tournament in every semesters is a tradition of MCC. Universities from different States come to participate in the tournament.
